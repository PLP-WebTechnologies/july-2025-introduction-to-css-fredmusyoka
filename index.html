About This Page

This page demonstrates the CSS Box Model with visible padding, borders, and margins.

Margin

Margin is the space outside the border, creating separation from other elements.

Padding

Padding is the space inside the border, between content and the edge.

Border

The border wraps around padding and content, defining the visible edge of an element.
